Humanforce to iCal

This service will create an iCal URL from your Humanforce roster. This URL can then be provided to Google Calendar or other calendar software.

This service has been designed to not store any credentials. Your Humanforce credentials will be encrypted, then embedded in the iCal link you are given. No user data of any kind is stored. If you ever want to stop using the service, simply discard your link.
